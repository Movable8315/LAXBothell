Summer Lacrosse Camp

📅 August 3–7, 2025 · Canyon Park Middle School

Best Lacrosse Camp on the East side! Join us this summer for the Cougar Lacrosse Camp, where players of all skill levels will sharpen their game, build confidence, and have a blast with top-tier coaching in a fun, supportive environment.

Our camp focuses on developing fundamental skills, game awareness, and teamwork — all while fostering a love for the game. Whether you're picking up a stick for the first time or looking to elevate your play, this is the perfect opportunity to grow as an athlete and teammate.

Support Bothell Lax

Sponsorship Opportunities

The Bothell Lacrosse Club offers opportunities to companies and individuals who can generously provide donations to support the Bothell lacrosse program. Sponsorship provides your organization with valuable exposure and brand recognition across the Bothell Lacrosse community. We also accept donations and will provide the necessary tax documentation.

As a non-profit organization, we strive to ensure that participation in lacrosse is available to all players from kindergarten through high school regardless of economic circumstances. As a result of donations from generous sponsors, we continue to drive down the cost of participation and provide financial scholarships to those in need.
