Lacrosse: Team Spirit & Excitement

Bothell Lacrosse Club is a volunteer-run, non-profit organization growing the sport of lacrosse in the Northshore community.

The sport

Join the Excitement of Lacrosse

Lacrosse is a fast-paced, adrenaline-filled sport that combines the precision of passing and shooting with the physicality of running and defending, making it one of the most exciting games to watch and play. Known for its quick transitions and thrilling goals, lacrosse is rapidly growing in popularity across the nation, attracting players of all ages.

With its deep roots in Native American history, the sport has evolved into a modern favorite, now gaining global recognition as one of the newest Olympic sports. As lacrosse continues to expand, it offers athletes unparalleled opportunities to compete at the highest levels.
